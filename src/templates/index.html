Hello there!

Another Euro, another occasion to pit cloudster against each other for the title of the ultimate football clairvoyant! Think that person is you? If so, join us in the Cloudreach Euro 2016 predictions game!

The game will run two concurrent competitions:

The sweepstake competition

No skills invovolved here. Each participant will be allocated one of the 24 participating countries. You win if your team wins the Euros! However, there's a twist... As we will likely have more than 24 cloudsters participate, you might have to share your winnings with other people. The game will ensure all teams have been allocated at least once before making them available again for a further round of allocations. Choose you moment wisely to enter the competition...

The score prediction competition

Are you the next Paul the Octopus? In this competition, you will be required to predict the scores for each game of the Euro 2016 group stage. You will:

  • Earn 1 point for each game where you predict the correct winning team
  • Earn 3 points for each game where you predict the correct exact scoreline

The stakes

There is a £3 entry fee for entering the game. The money will be used to make up the prizes as follows:

  • £1 will go to the sweepstake competition pot. The winner(s) get the full pot.
  • £2 will go the predictions competition pot. The pot will then be devided as follows:
    • 60% to the winner
    • 30% to runner up
    • 10% to the 3rd placed participant
The money can be given in either cash or by PayPal (more details to follow).

By clicking the button below, you agree to entering the game and contributing £3 towards the prizes.
